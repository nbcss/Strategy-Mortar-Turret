Version: 1.2.0
Date: 06. 11. 2025
  Features:
    - Added Heavy mortar turret, a 3x3 variant mortar turret with very long range and direct damage bonus, but has even lower shooting speed (15s -> 6s).
    - Added Light nuclear bomb, which can deal massive explosive damage without shock wave (but still could damage friendly structure).
  Changes:
    - Added Engine technology to mortar turret prerequisites.
    - Increase the research price of heavy shell, but reduced it's turret penalty time.
    - Added description text for all ammo technologies.
  Bugfixes:
    - Fixed shrapnel explosion sound was played at turret position
    - Fixed mortar turret was missing hit effect
    - Fixed heavy shell has longer turret cooldown penalty than expected
